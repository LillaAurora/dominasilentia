Privacy Policy

Domina Silentia is a showcase and community experience. We collect as little personal information as possible and treat your data with respect.

We use local storage solely to remember that you have acknowledged you are over 21 and, if you have passed the Silentis Test, to prevent repeated prompts. We do not use advertising or analytics cookies.

All responses you provide during the Silentis Test are used only to evaluate your suitability for entry. Once the evaluation is complete, we do not retain your responses for any other purpose.

This policy may evolve as we expand our services. If we make significant changes, we will notify you through the site. By using Domina Silentia, you agree to this privacy policy.

If you have questions or concerns about how we handle your data, please contact us.
